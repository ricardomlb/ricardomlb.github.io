Hi there! 

You have landed in my professional site, where you can find information 
about my career and projects. 

Currently, I work as a Senior Analytics Consultant at Ebiquity PLC, at least
that is the official title. If you wonder what it means, well, I use data to answer 
business questions for large firms, and large in this context just means they can afford the fee 
from which my pennies come from. Most of the time I find myself working on topics such as 
advertisement, prices and discounts, distribution, etc. 

Personally, I like to consider myself as an econometrician working 
in the private sector because the backbone of my job is to build statistical models and 
exploit their results. But before that, I started a career in academia 
doing a PhD in Economics, hence the econometrician in me. Nowadays, I spend little 
time researching, but I still do some. If you want to know more about my research activity, 
you can find working papers on this site.
